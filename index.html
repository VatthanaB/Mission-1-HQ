The Witcher

A captivating and intricate fantasy book series, The Witcher, penned by author Andrzej Sapkowski, weaves a tapestry of monster-slaying, political intrigue, and moral ambiguity. Set in a meticulously crafted medieval-inspired world, the series follows the journey of Geralt of Rivia, a mutant monster hunter known as a Witcher.

Atomic Habits

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear is a self-help book that explores the science of habit formation and provides practical strategies to build and sustain positive habits while breaking free from negative ones.
